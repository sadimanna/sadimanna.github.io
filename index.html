Dr. Siladittya Manna

Research Scientist & Self-Supervised Learning Expert

I am a passionate researcher specializing in self-supervised learning, computer vision, and medical image analysis. Currently working as a Research Scientist at Hong Kong Baptist University, I focus on developing innovative self-supervised approaches for various computer vision tasks including signature verification, medical imaging, and document analysis.

My research interests span self-supervised learning, federated learning, deep learning, computer vision, and medical image analysis. I am particularly interested in developing robust and interpretable AI systems that can learn meaningful representations without extensive manual annotations.

I completed my PhD focusing on advanced machine learning techniques and have published extensively in top-tier conferences and journals. My work has been cited over 124 times, reflecting the impact and relevance of my research contributions to the scientific community.
